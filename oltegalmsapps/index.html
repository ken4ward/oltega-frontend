Frequently Asked Questions

These are a list of our frequently asked questions, if noneof these addressyour convern kindly use our contact details. It's always our plesure to address your concerns as early as it should.

  • Is using the Online Virtual Learning Platform FREE?

    It is totally FREE for all schools on our platform with no additional cost. We did this to contribute to the schooling system for all communities.

  • How do I register my school and students?

    Please, click CREATE USER ACCOUNT at the top and enter the information as approprriate. The success indicator of your account creation will be diplayed on the login screen. A member of administrative team will contact you within 24 hours. Once approved, students can be uploaded or added.

  • I don't have a conventional school but have online students, can I register?

    Yes, you can. Provision has been made for all type of schools to adapt to the newest chnage created by the Coronavirus pandemic.

  • How do I go about setting up my school after registration?

  • I'm an individual not in school now, can I enrol in a school

    Definitely, yes. We want education to be ubuiquitous to the benefit of all. no matter the background, the class you stopped schooling, we will always link you up with the right school that meets your need.At the top, click CREATE STUDENT ACCOUNT, select MY SCHOOL when filling your details. A management team will reachout to you for enrollment.

  • I'm referred by school to join but do not know how to join the specific school, how do I?

    At the top, click CREATE STUDENT ACCOUNT, select the specific school name when filling your details. The school management team will reachout to you for enrollment.
